Welding in Ms. Rommel's Room

James Hotchkiss and welding apprentices from John Deere came to Ms. Rommel's class so her students could experience an augmented reality system. While they were here, the students were able to control an excavator and a welding simulator. It was such a great experience that John Deere provided for the students!
